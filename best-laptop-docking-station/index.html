Quick answer

The CalDigit TS4 Thunderbolt 4 dock is our top pick for power users who need maximum connectivity and performance.

For most home offices, the Anker PowerExpand 10-in-1 is the best overall value; the Dell D6000 excels for multi-monitor professionals, while the UGREEN and Baseus hubs serve budget-conscious buyers and beginners, respectively.

A good docking station turns your laptop into a true desktop workstation. It lets you connect a monitor, keyboard, mouse, ethernet, and other peripherals through a single cable, while also keeping your laptop charged. This simplifies your setup and boosts productivity, especially if you frequently switch between desk and mobile work. When choosing a dock, start with your laptop’s ports. Thunderbolt 4 docks offer the fastest speeds and most versatile connectivity, ideal for creative pros and multi-monitor setups. USB-C hubs are more affordable and sufficient for most home-office tasks, but check power delivery and video output compatibility. Also consider how many ports you genuinely need, more isn’t always better if it adds bulk you don’t use.
